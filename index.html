The Values in Practice

The framework yields nine key practices when applied across our three fundamental relationships.

1. Know Yourself

Cultivate honest self-awareness; observe your thoughts, feelings, and patterns without judgment.

2. Understand Others

Witness others as they truly are; look beyond assumptions and projections.

3. Notice More

Observe the world attentively; pay attention to details, patterns, and deeper meanings.

4. Accept Yourself

Welcome your whole being with kindness; recognize your inherent worth.

5. Value Others

Acknowledge others as your equals; respond with compassion and respect.

6. Embrace Reality

Recognize the invitations in every moment; respond with gratitude.

7. Live Authentically

Align your actions with your deepest values; grow intentionally.

8. Connect Genuinely

Build relationships based on truth, mutual understanding, and shared joy.

9. Contribute Meaningfully

Offer your unique gifts to create positive change; participate in communities that amplify collective impact.

SEE: Perceive with Honesty

Observe life with awareness, curiosity, and wonder. The practice of seeing involves attending to reality as it is, inquiring deeply into its nature, and maintaining a sense of awe about its mysteries.

Attention

Stay fully present to what is, without filters or distortions. Notice without judgment.

Inquiry

Investigate with genuine curiosity; ask deeper questions and seek continuous education.

Awe

Notice the connections, patterns, and meaning beyond the surface; remain open to beauty and mystery.

LOVE: Connect with Delight

Embrace life with humility, kindness, and acceptance. The practice of loving involves recognizing your equality with others, responding with care, and saying "yes" to reality through gratitude and joy.

Humility

Recognize yourself as equal to, but no better than, others; acknowledge both your strengths and limitations with honesty.

Kindness

Respond with genuine care to both beauty and brokenness; serve with intention and generosity of spirit.

Acceptance

Accept reality and its offers. Savor the magic of everyday moments through affirmation, gratitude, and joy.

LEAD: Act with Purpose

Move forward with intention, creativity, and perseverance. The practice of leading involves aligning your actions with your values, embracing growth through experimentation, and developing resilience in the face of challenges.

Direction

Move forward guided by values, vision, and ethical wisdom; act with integrity even when it's difficult.

Evolution

Adapt and grow through emergent possibilities; embrace experimentation, play, and creative discovery as paths to growth.

Resilience

Remain steadfast in commitment despite obstacles; balance persistence with flexibility; take action rather than waiting for rescue.
