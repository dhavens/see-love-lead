Framework Overview

The See, Love, Lead Framework guides us through three essential life practices that form a path to wisdom in action.

SEE

Perceive with Honesty

Observe life with awareness, curiosity, and wonder. Notice what is truly present.

LOVE

Connect with Delight

Embrace life with humility, kindness, and acceptance. Appreciate reality as it is.

LEAD

Act with Purpose

Move forward with intention, creativity, and perseverance. Take action with direction.

Nine Core Values

Attention

Humility

Recognize yourself as equal to, but no better than, others; acknowledge both strengths and limitations.

Inquiry

Awe

Acceptance

Accept reality and its offers. Savor the magic of everyday moments through gratitude and joy.

Resilience

Remain steadfast in commitment despite obstacles; balance persistence with flexibility.
